# E-Commerce Sales Analysis

## 📌 Project Overview

This project analyzes an e-commerce dataset to evaluate business performance, customer behavior, and product trends. The analysis was conducted using SQL for data extraction and Excel for building an interactive dashboard.

The goal is to transform raw transactional data into clear, actionable business insights.

---

## 🛠 Tools & Technologies

* SQL (data querying and analysis)
* Microsoft Excel (dashboard and visualization)
* Pivot Tables & Slicers (interactive filtering)

---

## 📊 Key Performance Indicators (KPIs)

* **Total Revenue** – overall sales generated
* **Total Profit** – net business performance
* **Total Orders** – number of unique transactions
* **Average Order Value (AOV)** – revenue per order

---

## 🔍 Analysis Performed

### 1. Customer Analysis

* Identified top customers by revenue
* Segmented customers into High, Medium, and Low value groups
* Classified customers as Repeat vs One-Time
* Detected customers with above-average spending

---

### 2. Product & Category Analysis

* Ranked top products by revenue
* Analyzed revenue contribution by category (%)
* Identified high-performing and low-performing products

---

### 3. Time & Trend Analysis

* Analyzed monthly revenue trends
* Calculated month-over-month growth rate
* Identified peak and low-performing periods
* Evaluated consistency and volatility in sales performance

---

## 📈 Dashboard Features

* Clean and structured layout for easy interpretation
* KPI cards summarizing business performance
* Monthly revenue trend line chart (core performance indicator)
* Top products bar chart for ranking analysis
* Category contribution visualization
* Customer segmentation breakdown
* Interactive slicers (Category, Region, Segment)

---

## 💡 Key Insights

* Revenue shows an overall upward trend with noticeable fluctuations, indicating periods of growth and decline.
* Certain product categories contribute a significant share of total revenue.
* A small segment of customers generates a large portion of total sales.
* Sales peaks suggest possible seasonal demand patterns.
* Revenue variability indicates opportunities for improving consistency in performance.
