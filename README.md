## Key Business Insights
Revenue & Growth Dynamics: Total revenue reached $2.29M with a healthy 12.5% month-over-month growth trend. Profitability remains stable at $286K, driven by high-volume transactional activity (nearly 10k total orders).

Category Leadership: The Technology sector is the primary growth engine, accounting for 53% of total revenue. Flagship products like the Canon 2200 Copier ($61.6k) significantly outperform the broader inventory, suggesting a focus on high-value hardware is key to scaling.

Customer Segmentation Strategy: A massive 76.4% of revenue is generated by the "Low-tier" segment, indicating a high-volume, retail-heavy customer base. There is a significant opportunity to grow the "High" and "Medium" segments to increase the average order value (currently $230).

Seasonal Trends: Data reveals critical sales peaks in September and November, contrasting with a Q1 dip in February ($59k). This identifies a clear need for targeted marketing campaigns during the Q1 "off-season" to stabilize annual cash flow.
