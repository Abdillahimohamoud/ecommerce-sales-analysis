## Insights
Sales Performance
Revenue Driver: Revenue is primarily driven by the Technology category, which contributes $836,154 (the highest among all categories).

Growth Peak: The sales trend shows a massive growth spike in September ($307,650) and November ($352,461), suggesting strong year-end performance.

Low Point: Sales experienced a significant decline in February, hitting a yearly low of $59,751.

 Customer Insights
Segment Dominance: The Low-tier customer segment is the powerhouse of the business, contributing 76.4% of total revenue.

High-Value Concentration: While the "High" segment has the fewest customers (only 19), they contribute nearly $180,261 in revenue, showing high individual loyalty.

Product Trends
Star Product: The Canon 2200 Copier is the top-performing product by a wide margin, generating $61,600 in sales.

Category Mix: While Technology leads in dollars, Office Supplies and Furniture are nearly tied in their contribution, keeping the revenue stream diversified.
